A purebred Wideband O2 Controller with no excess. Spartan 2 is "14point7's" next generation Lambda Controller for the Bosch LSU 4.9 Wideband O2 sensor.

Spartan 2 is the first Wideband Oxygen Controller to use all extended temperature components, this means Spartan 2 offers the widest operating temperaute; -40[C] to 125[C], of any Wideband Oxygen Controller on the market. From the frozen Arctic Tundra to the baking heat of Death Valley, Spartan 2 is the Wideband Oxygen Controller you can rely on.

Automotive qualified components offer increased reliability, increased resistance to electromagnetic interference, and exceptional durability when compared to non-automotive grade components.
