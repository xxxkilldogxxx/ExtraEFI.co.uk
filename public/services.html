Upgrades:

I'm no longer selling the daughter boards for MS1, MS2 or MS3 ECUs on their own due to a few customers having issues with installing them, themselves.

I now offer an upgrade service, where-by I will test the ECU, report back to you with any issues it may have and upgrade the main processor to which system you require.

Price includes minor mods needed to drive existing setups, e.g. EDIS wire changes inside, etc.


MS1 to MS2 Upgrade is £115 plus postage.

MS1 to MS3 Upgrade is £225 plus postage

MS2 to MS3 Upgrade is £200 plus postage.
